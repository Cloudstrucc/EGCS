You must choose a secure sign-in method to be able to access the Industrial Security Sector Portal.


  • Use the same sign-in information you use for other online services (for example, online banking) to sign-in to the Portal. You will temporarily go to your sign-in partner site; once signed in; you will be redirected back here.
  • Your sign-in partner site will not know which government service you are using. None of your information (for example, financial, banking) will be shared with the Impact Assessment Agency of Canada.
  • Sign-in with a GCKey user ID and password; or
  • Register for a GCKey user ID and password if you do not have one.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


•Don't share passwords or PINs online or over the phone.
•Don’t choose to have your credentials saved by your web browser.
•Log off or sign out of accounts and websites when you leave the computer.
•Clear your browser’s cache and close your browser after your online session.
•Use caution when entering passwords or PINs in public.
