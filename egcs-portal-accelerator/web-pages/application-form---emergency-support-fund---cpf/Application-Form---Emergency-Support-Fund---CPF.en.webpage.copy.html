Supporting Documents

The following attachments must be uploaded and included before your application can be submitted:

  • Proof of legal status (articles of incorporation, letters patent, partnership agreement or other documents)
  • Proof of signing authority (Board resolution or other document)
  • Permission to submit on behalf of the Authorized Representative (attestation, email, letter, etc.) (if applicable)
  • Direct deposit enrollment information (void cheque OR completed Direct Deposit Enrollment form stamped by your financial institution)

Failure to submit a duly completed application may result in delays in the processing of your application.
 

Audits


Canadian Heritage conducts audits on a sample of successful applications each year; the Department will assume the audit cost. In such cases, recipients must make available any records, documents, or other information that may be required to perform the audit. Recipients must retain for at least five years supporting documents related to the information provided in all application forms.

A recipient found to have submitted false or unsupported information may be required to repay the full amount of the financial support received, and may be declared ineligible from the Canada Periodical Fund for the next two fiscal years or more.

Helpful Hints

  • Click the Save button at the bottom of the application form at any time to save your application, and come back to it later if needed. Once saved, you will be able to access your active application under the My Applications section of the Menu at the top.
  • Under the My Applications section of the Menu at the top, you are able to see all of the funding requests your organization has submitted. Click on “Details” to access your application to complete, edit, or view your submission. You will be unable to make any changes to your application once the application has been submitted.
  • If you are experiencing difficulty submitting your application through this online portal, please contact the Department of Canadian Heritage.
 

Privacy Notice


The personal information you provide on this form will be used to administer your organization's funding application. The information is collected under the authority of the Department of Canadian Heritage Act . The personal information will be retained for seven years. Under the Privacy Act you have the right of access to, and correction of, your personal information. To exercise either of these rights, contact Canadian Heritage’s ATIP Coordinator by email at pch.aiprp-atip.pch@canada.ca. If you are not satisfied with Canadian Heritage’s response to your privacy concern, you may wish to contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada by telephone at 1-800-282-1376.
