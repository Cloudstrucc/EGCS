The CH-COVID-19 System to support COVID-19 funding requests (hereinafter “CH-COVID-19 system”) is an initiative of the Department of Canadian Heritage and as such, is owned and administered by the Department.

The purpose of this system is to permit eligible organizations to:

  1. create an online account by way of a registration process;
  2. submit one or more applications for funding assistance, and provide any required documentation electronically;
  3. manage account profile information and check on the status of one or more funding requests; and
  4. submit documents required by the terms and conditions of an approved funding agreement.

  1. Your acceptance of and compliance with these Terms;
  2. The authentication of your organization; and
  3. The authentication of your title/role within the applicant organization, as well as your authority to represent this organization. If you are the Primary Officer (see definition below) of your organization, your individual identity will need to be authenticated and authorization will be required from the head of your organization to complete the registration process.
